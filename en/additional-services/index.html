Frequently asked questions

Are these services included in your rental support package?
Some services such as SIG referral and insurance advice are included in our standard support. For premium services (concierge, tailored guarantee solutions), we provide a personalised quote based on your needs.
Can I access these services without a N20 rental contract?
Yes. Our additional services are available independently of a property search. Whether you found your home through another channel or simply wish to benefit from our partner network, contact us directly.
How far in advance should SIG contracts be activated?
We recommend initiating SIG procedures at least 2 weeks before your move-in date. This allows time to process the request and ensure activation for day one. N20 handles this coordination on your behalf.
What rental guarantee solutions do you offer?
We work with several specialists in Swiss rental guarantees, particularly for expat and international civil servant profiles. Solutions vary according to your situation: joint guarantee, bank guarantee or guarantee insurance.
