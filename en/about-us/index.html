Frequently asked questions

What are your fees for commercial premises?
Our fees for commercial premises correspond to 1 month's gross rent, with a minimum of CHF 5,000. They are payable only upon lease signature. No registration fee or prior commitment required.
Do you offer preferential rates for bulk relocations?
Yes. For companies relocating several employees simultaneously (3 or more), we offer negotiated fee structures and a dedicated contact. Please contact us for a personalised quote.
Do you assist employees with administrative procedures?
Yes. Our relocation service includes support with residence permits, bank account opening, school enrolment and procedures with local authorities. We can also recommend specialist legal partners.
Do you work directly with HR departments?
Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with the HR and Mobility teams of large organisations. We can establish a framework partnership agreement to simplify processes and guarantee consistent conditions for all your employees.
