Frequently asked questions

What types of properties do you offer for purchase?
We work across all residential segments: apartments, villas, houses, investment properties and prestige properties. Through our network, we access off-market properties not listed on public portals.
How does the valuation process work for selling?
We carry out a comparative market analysis based on recent transactions in your area, the condition of the property, its characteristics and the current Geneva market context. This valuation is free and non-binding.
Can I sell my property confidentially?
Yes. We offer confidential sales: your property is presented exclusively to qualified buyers within our network, without being listed on public portals. Ideal for sensitive transactions or prestige properties.
What are your fees for buying or selling?
Our fees are set on a case-by-case basis according to the nature and complexity of the transaction. They are always established in writing before we begin and are payable. Contact us for a personalised quote.
