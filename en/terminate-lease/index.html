Frequently Asked Questions

How does lease termination work with N20?
You contact us with details of your property (surface area, rent, desired departure date). We broadcast the listing in our network, select the best candidates, organise viewings and submit the chosen file to your landlord or property manager.
How long does a lease termination take?
The timeline varies depending on demand for your type of property and neighbourhood. In general, we find a qualified replacement tenant within 2 to 6 weeks. Since Geneva has a tight market, timelines are often short for well-located properties.
Do I need to inform my landlord before contacting N20?
We recommend first checking your lease agreement and informing your landlord or property manager of your intention to terminate. Contact us at the same time — we will guide you through the steps and advise you on the legal notice periods.
What types of property do you handle?
We handle all types of residential properties: studios, apartments of all sizes, duplexes and houses. For commercial premises, please contact us directly to assess feasibility.
