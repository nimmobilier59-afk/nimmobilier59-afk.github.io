Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our housing search service in Geneva.

Why use N20 Immobilier for your housing search?
+

N20 Immobilier specialises in relocation in Geneva and has deep knowledge of the local market, neighbourhoods, and Swiss-specific requirements (applications, permits, agency practices). You save considerable time and avoid the common pitfalls of searching alone in a very tight market. Our team guides you every step of the way: defining your needs, shortlisting properties, organising viewings, preparing your application file, and following through to key handover.

How much does your service cost?
+

Our service is only charged upon successful lease signing: 10% of the annual rent including charges for residential properties (min. CHF 1,500), or 1 month's rent for commercial premises (min. CHF 5,000). Registration is completely free — Fees apply only upon success.

Which Geneva neighbourhoods do you search?
+

N20 Immobilier covers the entire canton of Geneva: city centre, Eaux-Vives, Carouge, Champel, Grand-Saconnex, Meyrin, Lancy, Vernier, as well as the French border communes (Ferney-Voltaire, Saint-Genis-Pouilly, Annemasse). We tailor the search to your priorities: international schools, proximity to your workplace, budget, neighbourhood atmosphere.

What documents are needed to rent in Geneva?
+

To build a strong rental application in Geneva, you will need: a valid identity document or passport, a residence permit (or proof of application in progress), your last 3 pay slips or employment contract, an extract from the debt enforcement register. N20 supports you in building your application with complete peace of mind.

Do you assist expats and international civil servants?
+

Yes, this is one of our core specialisations. We regularly assist expats on assignment, civil servants from international organisations (UN, WHO, ICRC, WTO) and their families. We understand the specific constraints: tight arrival timelines, high-standard accommodation, proximity to international schools, and accompanying partner support.
